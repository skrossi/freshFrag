We at Fresh Fragrance Products Nagpur are fully capable of manufacturing & supplying pouches of different orientation, three side seal pouches, standing pouches, center seal pouches with spout & zipper. Fresh Fragrance Products have pioneered itself to produce pouches to fill air freshener contents (Powder or Gel Form) which is under Home Hygiene products given a trusted replacement for TYVEK Paper.

We have developed structure indigenously to fulfill all requirements for aroma diffusion through our inner pouches & outer pouches developed in such a manner to hold aroma of inner pouches until it is opened.

We are capable of serving the customer needs with firm commitments under the pledge to serve better on each & every supply we make.

Our Infrastructure

INFRASTRUCTURE

Fresh Fragrance Products work facility located centrally in India. Serving our customer EAST – WEST – NORTH – SOUTH with time-bound service.

WORK AREA

Fresh Fragrance Products has a roof setup area of 12,000 square feet with all its amenities & machinery required to produce air freshener pouches under one roof.

MACHINERY

A nine-color flexo printer in-line registration control with UV ink curing facility. State-of-art polymer plate mounting machine secures a high level of registration control by imparting a register image while printing. Equipped with two pouching machines to produce pouches with breathable paper & starch-based breathable film.

QUALITY

To deliver a quality pouch at each cut, we have designated a research & development team along with a sophisticated laboratory setup. Each air freshener's perfume needs proper diffusion with breathable paper & breathable film.
